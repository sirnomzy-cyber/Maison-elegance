Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly — name, email, shipping address, and payment details — as well as browsing behavior on our site to improve the shopping experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to process orders, provide customer support, personalize recommendations, and, with your consent, send marketing communications.

3. Sharing Your Information

We share information only with service providers necessary to fulfill orders (payment processors, shipping carriers) and never sell personal data to third parties.

4. Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences, maintain your shopping bag, and understand site usage. You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. Contact privacy@maisonelegance.com to exercise these rights.

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your information, including 256-bit SSL encryption at checkout.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be directed to privacy@maisonelegance.com.
