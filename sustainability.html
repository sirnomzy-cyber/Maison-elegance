Responsible Sourcing

Every material supplier is reviewed annually against labor, environmental, and traceability standards before renewal.

Ethical Manufacturing

We work exclusively with ateliers that meet or exceed regional fair labor standards, verified through independent audit.

Reduced Waste

Small-batch production and made-to-order options reduce excess inventory and end-of-season markdowns.

Recycled Packaging

Garment boxes and mailers are made from FSC-certified, recycled, and fully recyclable materials.

Carbon Reduction

Air freight is reserved for express orders only; the majority of shipments move by sea and ground.

Supplier Transparency

Our supplier list and audit summary are published annually in our impact report.

Looking Ahead

Our 2028 Goals

100% traceable material sourcing across all product categories.

Garment take-back and resale program across flagship boutiques.

In the Community

Community Initiatives
