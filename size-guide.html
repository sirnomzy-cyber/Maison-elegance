How to Measure

Bust/Chest: Measure around the fullest part of the chest, keeping the tape level.

Waist: Measure around the natural waistline, at the narrowest point of the torso.

Footwear: Measure foot length in the evening, when feet are at their largest, and refer to the millimeter chart on each product page.
