Shipping & Returns

Shipping

Standard Shipping — Complimentary on all orders. 5–8 business days.

Express Shipping — $35 flat rate. 2–3 business days.

International Shipping — Available to over 40 countries. Duties and taxes are calculated at checkout where applicable.

Boutique Pickup — Complimentary at select flagship locations; ready within 24 hours.

Order Tracking — A tracking link is emailed once your order ships. You can also track anytime via Track Order.

Packaging — Every order arrives in a reusable garment box with acid-free tissue, wrapped for the unboxing to feel like part of the experience.

Returns & Exchanges

Return Window — 30 days from the delivery date, on unworn items with original tags attached.

How to Return — Start a return from your Order History, or contact client care for a prepaid return label.

Exchanges — Free size or color exchanges within the same return window, subject to availability.

Refund Timeline — Refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5–7 business days of the return being received.

Final Sale Items — Items marked Final Sale, gift cards, and personalized pieces are not eligible for return.
